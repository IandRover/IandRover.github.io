About Me (CV)

Hi,

I am Chia-Jsoamg Kao. I am interested in model robustness, representation learning, and explainable AI. My goal is to develop robust and interpretable machine learning algorithms and systems that operate reliably even under challenging conditions.

I was fortunate to work with Prof. Wei-Chen Chiu (NYCU) and Dr. Pin-Yu Chen (MTI-IBM Watson AI Lab). Before that, I worked with Prof. Li-Fen Chen (NYCU) and Prof. Chih-Chieh (Albert) Yang (NYCU).

I am admitted to the CS Ph.D. program at Cornell University (Fall, 2022). However, I deferred my enrollment for one year as I have to complete my compulsory military service.
